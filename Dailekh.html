Introduction

Dailekh District a part of Karnali Province, is one of the 77 districts of Nepal. The district, with Dailekh as its district headquarters, covers an area of 1,502 km2 and had a population of 225,201 in 2001 and 261,770 in 2011.

History

Dailekh District was a part of Khasa kingdom during 12th to 14th century . Sinja Valley was the ancient capital city and powerful town of the Khasa Kingdom .After the fall down of the Khasa Kingdom it divided into many small kingdoms. Before the unification of modern Nepal, the area of the Karnali region had a united kingdom named Baise Rajya . Dailekh principality was one in 22 principalities.
