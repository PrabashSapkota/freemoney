Introduction

Rukum district is one of the seventy-seven districts of Nepal located in the Karnali Province. Rukum is known for its natural beauty and breathtaking landscapes, including the Sisne Himal mountain range and the Rukumkot hill, which offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.
