Historical Sites of Dailekh

Dullu

Dullu is an urban municipality of Dailekh District. Various cultural backgrounds have been settled in this place sharing their culture and lifestyle. Grand castle of once ‘Dullu Durbar’ is located right at dullu bazaar. The sight of the castle’s remaining, stone sculptures, pillars, and oldest script in Nepali is associated with the destination. Other attractions of Dullu include Dhuleshwar temple and Jain Tirthankara Mahabir of Paduka temple established back in the 18th century.

Mahabu

Mahabu is famous for ‘Mahabu Dham’ where people from different destinations visit for events held in the Dhaam. Mahabu valley is a hill covered destination with a beautiful tiny house and rhododendron existence. The destination is also famous for paragliding. Paragliding is done from Mahabu observing the beautiful hills to Gitachaur. Other attractions include Chamgaat river, Taulekhar Cave, Musekhola Waterfall, Shivalaya Temple.

Chamunda

This beautiful village is covered with forests, hill and agricultural land. Chamunda is also famous for its old cultural sites like Chamundadevi Temple, Bhairav Temple, Purnamala Temple, and Debathi Temple. The local people here follow Hinduism and Buddhism. Jal cave is also one of the favorite spots to visit for the visitors. Besides this, Chamunda is known for the polite hospitality of local people and good treatment by nature itself.

Bindrasaini

Bindrasaini is famous for its culture. Local people of this village are welcoming and polite in nature; their hospitality will surely be one of the best experiences for a lifetime. Dholak dance of local communities, art culture, and old authentic lifestyle of them will drag the attention of many travelers. Besides that, Bindrasaini is also good in natural existence. Bindrasaini temple, waterfall, rivers will surely catch your eyes during your travel.
